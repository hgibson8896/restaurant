Welcome to the Ocean Lake Pub

Ocean Lake Pub, Pasadena is a modern restaurant in a historic landmark. Located off the coast of scenic Coronado Bay, the relaxed yet upscale atmosphere inspires culinary delights. The ambiance embraces the beauty of waterfront dining alongside an industrial design concept.

Master Chef Cheryl Chop was inspired by classic California cuisine when she developed this delightful menu. Embrace the luxury of coastal dining at its finest. We offer intimate balconies that are ideal for small parties, and a large dining room to accommodate large parties or events.
